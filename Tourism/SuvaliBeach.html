Suvali Beach

Suvali Beach

About The Place : A tranquil and serene beach, Suvali Beach, is located 20 kilometres from Surat. The beach, which is primarily frequented by loners seekers of solitude, is steadily becoming a popular tourist destination. The spotless beach, which is outside the city centre, provides city dwellers and other tourists with the ideal escape from the shrill noise and hectic pace of the metropolis.
