Nilambag Palace

About The Place : In the hectic commercial and industrial city of Bhavnagar, Nilambag Palace Hotel comes as a pleasant juxtaposition with its cres of lush green lawns and beautiful foliage surround the imposing Khakhi stoned structure. Built in 1859 A.D., this relic of the past, home to the ruling Gohil dynasty of Bhavnagar, now stands as a Heritage Hotel.

Facilities:




  • Luxury room accommodation (room have large & Twin A/C bedrooms with well-appointed with attached bathroom bathtub/Tiled bathroom).
  • Break Fast, Lunch and Dinner (Veg. & Non.Veg.) Subject to including meal plan.
  • Free use of swimming pool, Health club and tennis at the exercise.
  • Tea and coffee maker in palace room.
  • Hot and cold running water.
  • Multi cuisine restaurant. (Indian, continental, Chinese and sea Food) Veg. / Non Veg.
  • Doctor on call and steam bath with minor charges.
