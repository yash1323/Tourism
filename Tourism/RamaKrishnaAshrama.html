RamaKrishna Ashrama

About the location: In the mid 19th century, Ramakrishna Paramhans set out to revitalize Vedic philosophy and principles amongst the people of India. Following his steps, the Ramakrishna Ashrama, Rajkot, located on a serene campus offers a value based education, has a public library and an Ayurvedic center, plus guest house and a permanent exhibition showcasing the life of Shri Ramakrishna Paramhans.
